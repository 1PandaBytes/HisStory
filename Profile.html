Meet William

You have been allocated William Malcolm, a young Australian lad of 16 years. He was born in 1900 and is of a middle class, home to demanding parents. He is currently completing his final year of school and is undergoing trials for university. However, at the moment, Australia is currently involved in a war with the Turks, taking place in Gallipoli. Pressure is steadily increasing for young men to enlist, those who do not are viewed as selfish and cowardly. William's parents care greatly about face despite their son being clearly underage, and their ambitions are ready to take a toll on the final years of his boyhood.
